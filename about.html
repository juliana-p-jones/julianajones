About:

A quick glance at my life and experiences, and a sneak peak at my resume.

Background:

After graduating the University of Delaware in 2018 as a first generation college student with a Bachelor's in Fine Arts, I worked as a graphic designer for a record label in Philadelphia. While learning more about the creative industry I found out about Year Up. I immediately knew this was an opportunity like no other, and left the VERY boring world of musicians and artists to join the tech community. I am so glad I did. I have begun working towards a career in application development, and I just know my creative background will help set me apart. I am so excited for this pivot and next chapter in my life.

As a vocal human rights activist, I've always aspired to give back to the community, so it is a dream of mine to head into the technology field with that as a core motivator. As a newcomer to the STEM community, I am overwhelmed in the best way possible by all the different avenues available in this industry to give back and make the world a better place.
